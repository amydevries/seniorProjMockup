About us!

The Shadowing program is a semester long practical work between WiCSE and local companies. Students will work with highly skilled engineers on teams of different disciplines to develop broader technical knowledge and industry expertise. WiCSE will have periodic review sessions with both participants and their teams to evaluate progress in the program.
